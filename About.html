About Juansa limited

Juansa Limited offers cleaning and janitorial services for private and public spaces catering for commercial, residential and industrial cleaning needs. Janitorial services include but not limited to daily cleaning, post construction cleaning, after party cleaning, detailed car and aeroplane wash, garbage collection, gardening, pest control and fumigation. We cater for the needs of our clients giving them quality services. Juansa Limited uses ecologically friendly cleaning products that are safe for the environment and clients
